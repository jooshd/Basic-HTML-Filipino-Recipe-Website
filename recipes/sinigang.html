Sinigang

Sinigang is a versatile Filipino dish that can warm anyone up on a cold winter day. It is made with fresh garden vegetables in a sweet and sour tamarind soup base. What makes it so versatile? This soup can work with any protein. Seafood, beef, pork, tofu. You name it, it can probably go in this soup and taste amazing. Want to learn about the pork recipe?
