Chicken Adobo

Chicken Adobo is a Filipino dish where chicken is marinated in soy sauce, vinegar, pepper, bay leaves, garlic, and other ingrediants before being seared and simmered until the chicken is fall off the bone. This is one of my favorite Filipino foods. Here is the link to learn more about the recipe!
